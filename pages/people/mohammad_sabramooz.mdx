Mohammadreza is a PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Toronto, supervised by Prof. J.J. Garcia-Luna-Aceves. His research focuses on security for routing protocols in computer networks, with an interest in applying cryptographic techniques to build verifiable and trustworthy network infrastructure.

Prior to the University of Toronto, he completed his Master's in Information Technology Engineering at École de technologie supérieure (Université du Québec), where his work focused on robust federated learning and blockchain-based credential revocation. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Sharif University of Technology.

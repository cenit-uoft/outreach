Lindsay M. Montgomery is an Associate Professor of Anthropology and Indigenous Studies at the University of Toronto St. George campus.  She holds a B.A. (cum laude) in Human Rights & Anthropology from Columbia University and a PhD in Anthropology from Stanford University. Her research and teaching draws on feminist, collaborative, and anti-colonial approaches to documenting long-term land use practices and histories of inter-ethnic interaction in the American West.

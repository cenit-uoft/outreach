Tailai Song received his Ph.D. in Electrical, Electronics and Communications Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Michela Meo and Prof. Marco Mellia from Politecnico di Torino, Italy, in 2026. He is a member of the SmartData@Polito Research Center and the Telecommunication Networks Group. He also received his B.Sc. in Automotive Engineering and M.Sc. in ICT for Smart Societies from Politecnico di Torino in 2020 and 2022, respectively. His research lies at the intersection of computer networks, communication systems, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence. He has authored more than 15 papers published in established international conferences and journals.

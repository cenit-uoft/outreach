Mahdi’s research focuses on decentralized wireless networks and next-generation multiple access protocols for future communication systems. His work studies how wireless users can share the channel efficiently without centralized scheduling, with the goal of improving effective throughput, reliability, and scalability in increasingly distributed and connected networks.

Tsung Jen (Charles) Hsieh is a Master of Applied Science (MASc) student in the CENIT Research Group, where he focuses on the development and analysis of routing protocols within autonomous systems. He earned his Bachelor of Applied Science (BASc) degree with High Honours in Computer Engineering from the University of Toronto, with minor in Artificial Intelligence Engineering and certificate in Engineering Business. His emphasis is on the design and analysis of shortest-path routing protocols that are loop-free at every instance even in the midst of network changes.
